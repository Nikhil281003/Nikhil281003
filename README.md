**MoM — Meeting Intelligence**

Converts a raw audio recording into polished Minutes of Meeting — noise reduction, speaker diarization, transcript correction, and MoM generation in one async pipeline. Llama 8B handles three tasks simultaneously: summarisation, speaker extraction, and ASR correction across six languages including Hinglish. Five Docker containers, GPU-reserved, with independent per-service retry logic.

Takes a product demo video up to 60 minutes and produces structured technical documentation — workflows, feature descriptions, step-by-step guides. Async parallel frame and audio extraction makes it 65% faster than sequential and reduces manual writing effort by ~85%. The LLM fuses visual and audio context rather than treating them separately.
